Publication Process

Redelve International Publications provides Open Access to our journals and all our articles go through a diligent and thorough editorial and peer-review process in order to maintain the quality of content.

What is peer review?

Peer review is commonly accepted as an essential part of a scientific publication. It is the evaluation of work by one or more people of similar competence to the producers of the work. Peer review methods are employed to maintain standards of quality, improve performance, and provide credibility.

The peer review process
Publication Ethics
  1. Authors should refrain from misrepresenting research results which could damage the trust in the journal, the professionalism of scientific authorship, and ultimately the entire scientific endeavor.
  2. The manuscript has not been published previously (partly or in full) unless the new work concerns an expansion of previous work (please provide transparency on the re-use of material to avoid the hint of text-recycling (‘self-plagiarism’)).
  3. Authors should include Disclosure of potential conflicts of interest.
  4. Authors must disclose all relationships or interests that could have direct or potential influence or impart bias on the work. Although an author may not feel there is any conflict, the disclosure of relationships and interests provides a more complete and transparent process, leading to an accurate and objective assessment of the work.
  5. Examples of potential conflicts of interests may include but are not limited to the following :
  • Research grants from funding agencies (please give the research funder and the grant number)
  • Financial support for attending symposia
  • Financial support for educational programs
  • Support from a project sponsor
  • Publication Ethics and Rectitude
  • Conflicts of Interest
  • Confidentiality
  • Authorship
  • Article Modifications
  • Use of patient images or case details
  • Timely Publication of Content.

For any queries or any discussions regarding Publication Ethics, please contact us at: contact@redelve.com
