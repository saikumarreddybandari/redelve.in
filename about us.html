About us

Who are we?

ReDelve is an international, independent publisher of academic and professional research. We provide Open Access to our journals and all our articles go through a diligent and thorough editorial and peer-review process in order to maintain the quality of content. We set high standards for ourselves, so we filter and provide the best of research papers.

Our Mission:

In this new age of constant technological innovation, information is the key to all advancement. Our goal is to provide genuine research in an organized way to make it accessible to a greater number of people worldwide. In turn, we hope that this accessibility can inspire and propagate novel discoveries and inventions in a multitude of fields. We believe passionately that encouraging novel ideas and research will help improve society and make a difference in the world, however small it may be.

What we do?

Our highly qualified editorial management team and IT support team work around the clock to help deliver quick and efficient publishing results. Our team thrives to provide distinctive assistance specific to the needs of researchers, doctors, students, academicians and institutions.
