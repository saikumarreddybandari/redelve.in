Redelve Membership

ReDelve membership allows scientists, researchers, educators & industry professionals to publish at exclusive member rates. Our membership program allows authors (who submit articles frequently) to avail special benefits such as flexibility in submission dates and specialized editorial support.

  • Individual membership - for scientists, researchers, educators & industry professionals
  • Institutional membership - for universities, research/educational institutes, societies & associations
  • Corporate membership - for industries, companies & corporations

Individual membership:

Institutional membership is for universities, research/educational institutes, societies & associations.

  • Half year membership - Member can submit 6 manuscripts to any ReDelve journal
  • Annual membership - Member can submit 11 manuscripts to any of the ReDelve journal

Institutional membership:

Institutional membership is for universities, research/educational institutes, societies & association

  • Half year membership - Registered University/Institute can submit 6 number of articles to any ReDelve journal
  • Annual membership - Registered University/Institute can submit 16 manuscripts to any the ReDelve journal

Corporate membership:

Corporate membership is for industries, companies & corporations.

  • Half year membership - Registered Organization can submit 10 articles to any ReDelve journals
  • Annual membership - Registered Organization can submit 20 articles to any ReDelve journal

Membership Fees:

For any queries regarding our Membership Program, please contact us at: contact@redelve.com
