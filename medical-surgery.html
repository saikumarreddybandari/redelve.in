Surgery and Anesthesiology Research

View Editorial Board Members

Surgery and Anesthesiology Researchis an international, online, open access, peer-reviewed journal that aims to publish research, original papers, reviews, mini reviews, case reports, case series, case studies, perspectives, erratum, short communications etc. The journal aims to spread the recent studies and information; from the entire field of Surgery and Anesthesiology to the scientific community. It strives to provide a unique forum for the exchange of knowledge and information about new and significant research in the field of Surgery and Anesthesiology.

Surgery and Anesthesiology Research aims to enlighten the readers and researchers with the innovations, new ideas, implementations, opinions, developments and key issues in the field of Surgery and Anesthesiology, clinical, practical and scientific interests, amongst all the researchers related to Surgery and Anesthesiology and its related branches across the world.

Manuscript Submission
Author’s should go through the instructions before the submission and must fill the cover letter before submitting the article, which gives an opportunity for the authors to highlight the significance of their research and concept to journal editors. It also justifies the scope and relevance of the manuscript to the target journal. Submission can be done through Online Submission System or you can submit to submissions@redelve.com
